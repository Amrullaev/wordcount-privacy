Privacy Policy — Word Count

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how the Word Count application (“App”, “we”, “our”) handles and protects the privacy and personal information of its users.

1. General Information

Word Count is a mobile application designed for counting words, characters, and sentences in a text. The App performs all of its main functions completely offline.

2. Data We Collect

The Word Count App may process information only in the following case: the App processes and counts the text that you manually enter into your device. The App does not automatically collect personal data such as contacts, location, files, or device identifiers.

3. How We Use Data

The App uses your text solely for the following purposes: counting words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs; and displaying the result to the user through the interface.

4. Data Storage and Transmission

By default, Word Count does not send any data to the internet and does not store information on servers. If future optional features such as exporting or saving to cloud storage are introduced, this Policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Third-Party Services

The current version of the App does not integrate with any third-party services. If such services are added in the future, users will be notified, and this Policy will be updated.

6. Permissions

The App only requests permissions when necessary and only with explicit user consent. The current offline functionality does not require any permissions.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directly intended for children. If you are submitting information on behalf of a child under the age of 13, please ensure that parental consent has been obtained.

8. Security

We take standard industry security measures to protect user information. If any data transmission to servers is introduced in future updates, such data will be encrypted and stored with restricted access.

9. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Policy as needed. Any changes will be announced within the App or on its official page.

10. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: wordcount.dev.contact@gmail.com
